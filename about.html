Here you can find information about the site!

Vida's International Sweetshop was founded in 1997 by Vida Althought there arent any physical shops, all our stock can be purchase online since this site is dedicated to providing the most top quality sweets to our loyal costumers.

This business originally began as a bet between Vida and her friends, Vida's friends bet her $1 that she couldnt create a sweetshop and sell sweets to buying customers, Vida then took up the challenge and decided to make a small online sweetshop and thus began the booming business that is well known today as Vida's International Sweetshop. So basically she has her friends to thank.
